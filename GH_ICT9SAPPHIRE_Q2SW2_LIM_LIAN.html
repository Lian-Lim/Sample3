Shih tzu Shih tzu is a small but sturdy dog with a lush, long, double-hair coat. This breed's alert, confident, playful, and courageous demeanor makes it a favorite amongst toy dog enthusiasts and is an ancient breed and has a long history as a lap dog to nobles.
Corgi Corgi is a strong, athletic, and lively little herder who is affectionate and companionable without being needy. They are one the world's most popular herding breeds.
Husky Husky is a medium-sized dog, slightly longer than tall. Height ranges from 20 to 23 1/2 inches and weight from 35 to 60 pounds and the husky has erect ears and eyes of brown to blue or maybe even one of each color and the neck is carried straight and the topline is level.
